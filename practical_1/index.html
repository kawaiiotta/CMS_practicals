About NewBit

NewBit was founded amidst the uprising new regulations on personal data. Mainly in the EU personal data has become increasingly

where the general populous doesnt even know that there are other forms of data.

Join us and find out how you can create, find, and epsecially use other kinds of data in your business. We help you explore
your knowledge, store it for you in your personalized clouds, and anaylize it for you to consider in future business decisions.
